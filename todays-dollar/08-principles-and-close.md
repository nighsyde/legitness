# PART IX — PRINCIPLES CHEAT SHEETS

---

## Principle 1 — Assignment Before Appetite
Money gets a job before it gets a mood.

## Principle 2 — Rate Over Story
Savings rate, fee rate, interest rate, learning rate—rates run the future. Stories entertain the present.

## Principle 3 — Fence the Fun
Joy without a fence becomes a leak. A fence without joy becomes a rebellion.

## Principle 4 — Automate Solvent Self
Defaults should favor freedom. Heroes are for emergencies.

## Principle 5 — Slack Is Strength
Margin of safety in cash, time, and promises.

## Principle 6 — Usefulness Is an Investment
Today’s skill practice is a deposit.

## Principle 7 — Attention Is a Budget Line
What you stare at, you eventually fund.

## Principle 8 — Invert First
Delete paths to ruin before optimizing return.

## Principle 9 — Explainability Filter
If you can’t explain it, you can’t own it safely.

## Principle 10 — Repair Beats Purity
Missed days are normal. Secrecy and spirals are optional.

## Principle 11 — Peer Gravity Is Real
Choose rooms that make solvency normal.

## Principle 12 — Enough Ends Infinite Errands
Many daily temptations die when enough is defined.

# PART X — 30-DAY INSTALLATION PLAN

*Install the mind. Don’t binge the PDF and relapse.*

### Days 1–3 — See
- List accounts  
- Calculate rough runway months  
- Write Five Daily Questions on paper  

### Days 4–7 — Fence
- Create/label Freedom, Oxygen, Joy (and Craft if useful)  
- Set Joy weekly amount you can keep  
- Delete two temptation cues  

### Days 8–14 — Route
- Write payday protocol percentages  
- Automate Freedom transfer  
- Kill one high-interest behavior or balance action  

### Days 15–21 — Attract/Avoid
- Build your personal Attract list (10 items)  
- Build your Avoid list (10 items)  
- Practice purchase tree on every spend > threshold  

### Days 22–26 — Earn
- Daily skill block  
- One negotiation or proposal  
- One proof shipped publicly or to a customer  

### Days 27–30 — Review
- First Weekly Wealth Hour  
- Decision journal themes  
- Teach one person the payday script  
- Commit to the next 30 days at +1% surplus if possible

# PART XI — CLOSING

## The Point

You don’t become wealthy by admiring compound interest from far away.  
You become wealthy by **winning enough ordinary days** that compound interest finally has something to chew on.

So:

- Attract surplus, ownership, usefulness, runway, and calm rooms.  
- Avoid urgency, ratchets, identity debt, opaque complexity, and envy-as-advisor.  
- Every day: assign dollars, ship usefulness, remove a leak, settle at night.  
- On payday: route like an adult before you celebrate like a human.  

Do this until it is not a book you read but a reflex you trust.
