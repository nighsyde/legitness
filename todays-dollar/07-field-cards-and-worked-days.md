## Field Card D — Daily Loop

Morning: money events + intention

## Field Card E — Five Questions

1. What is this dollar for?  
2. Freedom ↑ or ↓ in 90 days?  
3. Attracted by value or by envy/fear/boredom/status?  
4. What would Solvent Self do in ten minutes?  
5. What can I automate to need less heroism tomorrow?  

## Field Card F — Purchase Pause

Large want → 30 days → freedom hours → bucket check → decide  

## Field Card G — Panic / Euphoria

Panic: runway → written plan → rule-based acts only  
Euphoria: pause → park → policy allocation → quiet celebration  

## Field Card H — Leak Hunt (10 minutes)

Open bank/credit → sort merchants → cancel one → renegotiate one → note one pattern

# PART VIII — WORKED DAYS

*Same principles. Different Tuesdays.*

---

## Day Type 1 — The Broke-Feeling Tuesday

You are not necessarily broke. You feel cornered.

**Do**
- Open runway number  
- List bills due in 14 days  
- Cut one nonessential outflow today  
- Earn/claim one receivable (invoice, extra shift ask, sale of unused item)  
- No new identity spending  

**Don’t**
- Gamble your way out  
- Shame-buy  
- Ignore the mail  

## Day Type 2 — The Payday Friday

**Do** the routing ceremony before friends suggest dinner theater.  
Fund Joy, then enjoy dinner from Joy without apology.

## Day Type 3 — The Envy Scroll Night

**Do** put the phone down; write the envy object; add to 30-day list; do a skill block or sleep.  
**Don’t** one-click.

## Day Type 4 — The Market Crash Morning

**Do** Field Card G.  
**Don’t** hold a referendum on your entire philosophy before breakfast.

## Day Type 5 — The Bonus Afternoon

**Do** Windfall Protocol. Tell almost nobody.  
**Don’t** upgrade housing in your head before the money is allocated.

## Day Type 6 — The Exhausted Should-Spend Day

Tired brains buy relief.

**Do** precommit: no purchases over $X when sleep < 6 hours.  
**Don’t** “treat yourself” with Freedom money.

## Day Type 7 — The Deep Work Day

**Do** protect the earning asset: attention.  
Batch money admin to the edge of the day.  
Ship usefulness. That is wealth work.
