# Today’s Dollar

### How to Think About Money So Wealth Is Built on Ordinary Days

A practical book on the **daily thought process** of wealth: what to do with money today, what should attract you, what to avoid, and how to route dollars before appetite speaks.

This is not another “wait 40 years” lecture. Compound interest still matters—this book teaches the days that feed it.

## The spine in four lines

1. **Assign every dollar a job before appetite speaks.**  
2. **Attract** surplus, ownership, usefulness, runway, calm rooms.  
3. **Avoid** urgency, ratchets, identity debt, opacity, envy-as-advisor.  
4. **Every day:** route or refuse, ship usefulness, remove a leak, settle at night.
