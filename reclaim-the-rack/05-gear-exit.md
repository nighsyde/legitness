## The only three paths

| Exit | When | Success looks like |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **Sell** | Still valuable; you will not use in 6 months | Listed, sold or price-dropped, gone |
| **Gift** | Useful to a student, nonprofit, friend, community lab | Handed off; receipt or thank-you optional |
| **Recycle / e-waste** | Dead weight, no safe buyer, hazardous | Dropped at proper e-waste; not “basement forever” |

**Forbidden:** floor maybes, “for parts” without a labeled bin + list, indefinite Decide after the tote date.

---

## Decision tree

```text
Will I use this in 6 months for a charter mission?
  NO → Does someone else need it more than I need the space?
         YES → Gift
         NO  → Is it worth > ~$X of my time to list? (pick X, e.g. $40)
                YES → Sell
                NO  → Recycle / e-waste
  YES → Keep home in rack or labeled bin (not floor)
```

After Decide tote deadline: unanswered items follow this tree automatically — usually Gift or Recycle if selling energy is gone.
