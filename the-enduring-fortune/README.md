### The Seven Laws (Spine of the Book)

1. Earn more than you spend—forever.  
2. Pay yourself first.  
3. Own assets that work while you sleep.  
4. Compounding needs time and uninterrupted capital.  
5. Risk that can ruin you is never worth the return.  
6. Character is the true balance sheet.  
7. Wealth without stewardship becomes waste within two generations.
